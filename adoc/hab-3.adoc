== Payload prep workshops

Valparaiso University, https://maps.app.goo.gl/phL8MvmFk1gyno58A[Gellersen building^].
Use the *east entrance on Sturdy Rd.* and park next to the silver oval solar energy research building.

* Thursday July 10 at 7:30 PM.

* Friday July 11 at 6:00 PM.

== Saturday July 12 Schedule

9:30 AM:: Participants arrive and check in.

10:00 AM:: Balloon filling will begin after payloads are attached. +
Count down and release will happen once all systems are GO for launch (about 10:20).

10:30 -- 11:30 AM:: (_approx._) Faculty-led tours of the College of Engineering building and the Department of Geography, Meteorology, and Environmental Science building.


== Where
